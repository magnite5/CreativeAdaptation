## Premise
Dorothy, (with Toto?) go through Oz, with the Player controlling Dorothy. 

## Levels
0. Tutorial
- In Kansas. The player learns how to play the game, while going to Mr. Marvel's caravan, then gets swept up with the house, ending with the house landing on top of the Witch.
1. Yellow Brick Road
- Player collects the slippers from the witch, leaves Toto Land 
- Player follows Yellow Brick Road
    - Meets Scarecrow
    - Meets Tin Man
    - Meets Lion
    - Witch Encounter: Fireballs
2. Emerald City & Path to Witch's Castle
- Enter the Emerald City
- Must find a way into the Wizard's chambers
- Get the Broomstick quest from the Wizard
- Walks through the Witch's forest + gets abducted by monkeys
